What is the Wimbledon Champions Board?

The Wimbledon Champions Board is a prestigious board that displays the names of all the winners of the Wimbledon Championships. It is located at the All England Lawn Tennis and Croquet Club in Wimbledon, London. The board is made of wood and features engraved plates with the names of the champions. Each plate includes the year of the championship and the name of the winner.

As mentioned earlier, the board dates back to the first Wimbledon Championships in 1877. Since then, the names of every winner, from the legendary Billie Jean King to the iconic Roger Federer, have been added to the board.
