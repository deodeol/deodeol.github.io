What are Milk Duds?

History and Myth of Milk Duds

Milk Duds were first introduced in 1926 by the Holloway Candy Company. The name "Milk Duds" was chosen to reflect the idea of a perfectly round candy that was "dud" or non-flawed. Over the years, Milk Duds have become a beloved treat, often enjoyed during movie nights or as a sweet indulgence.
