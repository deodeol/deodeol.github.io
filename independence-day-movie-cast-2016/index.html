The 2016 film "Independence Day: Resurgence" was highly anticipated by fans of the original movie, and it did not disappoint. The film featured a talented cast of actors who brought the story to life and made it a must-see for action movie enthusiasts. In this blog post, we will take a closer look at the cast of "Independence Day: Resurgence" and their roles in the film.

Introduction

"Independence Day: Resurgence" is a sequel to the 1996 film "Independence Day" and takes place twenty years after the events of the original. The film follows a new generation of heroes as they battle against a renewed alien threat. The cast of the film includes both returning actors from the original movie and new additions to the franchise.

Main Cast

The main cast of "Independence Day: Resurgence" includes the following actors:

  • Liam Hemsworth as Jake Morrison
  • Jeff Goldblum as David Levinson
  • Bill Pullman as President Thomas J. Whitmore
  • Maika Monroe as Patricia Whitmore
  • William Fichtner as General Joshua Adams
  • Charlotte Gainsbourg as Dr. Catherine Marceaux
  • Judd Hirsch as Julius Levinson
  • Brent Spiner as Dr. Brackish Okun
  • Vivica A. Fox as Jasmine Dubrow-Hiller

Supporting Cast

In addition to the main cast, "Independence Day: Resurgence" also features a talented supporting cast. Some of the notable actors in supporting roles include:

  • Sela Ward as President Elizabeth Lanford
  • Angelababy as Rain Lao
  • Deobia Oparei as Dikembe Umbutu
  • Travis Tope as Charlie Miller
  • Chin Han as Commander Jiang Lao

Conclusion

The cast of "Independence Day: Resurgence" brought their A-game to the film, delivering memorable performances that added to the excitement and thrill of the movie. From the returning actors who reprised their roles from the original film to the new additions who brought fresh energy to the franchise, each member of the cast contributed to the overall success of the film. If you're a fan of action-packed sci-fi films, "Independence Day: Resurgence" is definitely worth a watch.
