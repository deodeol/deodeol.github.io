What is Too Good To Go Australia?

Too Good To Go Australia is an app that aims to reduce food waste by connecting businesses with customers who want to buy leftover food at discounted prices. The app works by allowing businesses to list any surplus food they have at the end of the day, and customers can then purchase these items at a fraction of the original price. It's a win-win situation for both businesses and customers – businesses can reduce food waste and make some extra money, while customers can enjoy delicious meals at affordable prices.

The Story Behind Too Good To Go Australia

Too Good To Go was originally founded in Denmark in 2015 and has since expanded to multiple countries around the world, including Australia. The app was created with the goal of reducing food waste, which is a significant issue globally. In Australia alone, it is estimated that over 7.3 million tonnes of food is wasted each year. Too Good To Go Australia aims to combat this issue by providing a platform for businesses to sell their surplus food and for customers to purchase it at discounted prices.
