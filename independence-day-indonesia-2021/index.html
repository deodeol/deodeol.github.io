What is Independence Day Indonesia 2021?

Independence Day Indonesia 2021 marks the 76th anniversary of Indonesia's independence from colonial rule. On August 17, 1945, Indonesia's founding fathers declared independence, and the country began its journey towards self-governance.

History and Myth of Independence Day Indonesia 2021

The history of Independence Day Indonesia dates back to the colonial period when Indonesia was under Dutch rule. The struggle for independence began in the early 20th century and was led by prominent figures such as Sukarno and Hatta.

One of the myths surrounding Independence Day Indonesia is the story of the Proclamation of Independence. According to legend, Sukarno and Hatta drafted the proclamation document in a small house in Jakarta on August 17, 1945.
