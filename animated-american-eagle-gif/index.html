History and Myth of the American Eagle

The American Eagle has long been a symbol of freedom and strength in the United States. It is often associated with patriotism and national pride. The bald eagle, in particular, has been the official emblem of the United States since 1782, chosen for its majestic appearance and powerful presence. In Native American mythology, the eagle is seen as a messenger between the heavens and the earth, representing wisdom and spiritual insight.
