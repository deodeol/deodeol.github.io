How to Make Philadelphia Whipped Cream Cheese Ingredients

Making Philadelphia whipped cream cheese at home is a simple process. Here's a basic recipe to get you started: 1. In a mixing bowl, combine cream, milk, and whey protein concentrate. 2. Add salt and carob bean gum to the mixture and whisk until well combined. 3. Add cheese culture to the mixture and stir gently. 4. Cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel and let it sit at room temperature for 24 hours to allow the cream cheese to develop its flavor. 5. Once the cream cheese has set, transfer it to a food processor and blend until smooth and creamy. 6. Store the whipped cream cheese in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.

What If You Don't Have Philadelphia Whipped Cream Cheese Ingredients?

If you don't have Philadelphia whipped cream cheese on hand, you can still enjoy a creamy and delicious spread by making your own cream cheese at home. All you need is cream, lemon juice, and salt. Simply heat the cream until it reaches a simmer, then add lemon juice and salt. Stir gently and let the mixture sit for a few minutes until it starts to curdle. Strain the curds through a cheesecloth and let them drain for a few hours to remove any excess liquid. The result is a homemade cream cheese that can be used in all your favorite recipes.
