What is CTE Meaning Engineering?

CTE Meaning Engineering stands for Coefficient of Thermal Expansion. It is a measurement that quantifies how much a material expands or contracts with changes in temperature. This is an important property to consider in engineering, as it can affect the performance and durability of structures and components. By understanding the CTE of different materials, engineers can make informed decisions about material selection, design, and performance predictions.
