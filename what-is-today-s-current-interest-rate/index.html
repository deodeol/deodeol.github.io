The history and myth of interest rates

Interest rates have a long history, dating back to ancient civilizations. In ancient Greece and Rome, moneylenders would charge interest on loans, often at high rates. In modern times, interest rates are closely monitored and adjusted by central banks to maintain economic stability. While there are many myths and misconceptions surrounding interest rates, it's important to understand the facts and make informed decisions based on your financial goals and circumstances.
