Padres Batting Averages: A Closer Look

Everyone knows that batting averages are an important statistic in baseball, but what exactly do they mean? In simple terms, a batting average is calculated by dividing the number of hits a player gets by the number of at-bats they have. This gives you a decimal number, which is then multiplied by 100 to give you a percentage. The higher the percentage, the better the player is performing at the plate.
