Is Independence Day: Resurgence a Sequel?

Yes, Independence Day: Resurgence is indeed a sequel to the original Independence Day movie that was released in 1996. The film takes place 20 years after the events of the first film and continues the story of humanity's fight against an alien invasion. While some sequels fail to capture the magic of the original, Independence Day: Resurgence manages to stay true to the spirit of the first film while introducing new characters and expanding on the existing storyline.

What is Independence Day: Resurgence?

Independence Day: Resurgence is a science fiction action film that serves as a sequel to the original Independence Day. When the aliens do return with a larger and more powerful mothership, it is up to a new generation of heroes to save humanity once again.
