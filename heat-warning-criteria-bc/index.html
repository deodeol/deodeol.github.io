Recommendations for Heat Warning Criteria in BC

Now that we understand the heat warning criteria in BC, let's discuss some recommendations to stay safe during a heatwave. These recommendations are based on expert advice and are intended to help you protect yourself and your loved ones from the health risks associated with extreme heat.

1. Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water throughout the day to prevent dehydration. Avoid alcohol and caffeine, as they can dehydrate the body.

2. Stay cool: Seek air-conditioned spaces or use fans to cool down. If you don't have access to air conditioning, take cool showers or baths and use wet towels to cool off.

3. Dress appropriately: Wear lightweight, loose-fitting clothing that allows your body to breathe. Avoid dark colors, as they absorb heat.
