What are Padres Hitting Stats 2023?

Padres hitting stats for 2023 refer to the statistical measures that track the performance of the team's hitters throughout the season. These stats include batting average, on-base percentage, slugging percentage, home runs, RBIs, and more.

Batting average is a widely used hitting stat that measures a player's success at getting a hit per at-bat. Slugging percentage calculates the total bases a player earns per at-bat, taking into account singles, doubles, triples, and home runs.
