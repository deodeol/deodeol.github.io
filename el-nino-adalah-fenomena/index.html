What is El Nino Adalah Fenomena?

El Nino adalah fenomena is a natural climate phenomenon that occurs periodically in the central and eastern Pacific Ocean. It is characterized by warmer than usual sea surface temperatures and altered atmospheric pressure patterns. This phenomenon can have significant impacts on weather patterns and climate conditions worldwide.

El Nino is caused by the interaction between the ocean and the atmosphere. Normally, trade winds blow from east to west, pushing warm surface waters to the western Pacific. This causes upwelling of cold, nutrient-rich waters in the eastern Pacific, supporting a thriving ecosystem. However, during El Nino, these trade winds weaken, allowing warm waters to flow back towards the east. This disrupts the normal oceanic and atmospheric circulation patterns, leading to a cascade of effects on climate.
