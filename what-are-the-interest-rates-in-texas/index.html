Understanding Interest Rates in Texas and Related Keywords

Interest rates in Texas can be complex, but understanding the basics is crucial when it comes to borrowing money. Here are a few key terms related to interest rates:

Annual Percentage Rate (APR): The APR is the total cost of borrowing, including both the interest rate and any fees or charges associated with the loan.

Fixed-Rate Mortgage: A fixed-rate mortgage is a loan with an interest rate that remains the same for the entire term of the loan.

Adjustable-Rate Mortgage (ARM): An adjustable-rate mortgage is a loan with an interest rate that can change over time based on market conditions.

Prime Rate: The prime rate is the interest rate that banks charge their most creditworthy customers. It serves as a benchmark for other interest rates.
