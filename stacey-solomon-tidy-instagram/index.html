Q: How can I start decluttering my home?

A: Start by tackling one area at a time and ask yourself if each item brings you joy or serves a purpose. If not, it may be time to let go and donate or sell the item.

Q: How can I maintain an organized home?

A: Set aside a few minutes each day to tidy up and put things back in their designated places. Creating a routine and sticking to it will help you maintain a clutter-free home.

A: Utilize vertical space by installing shelves or hanging organizers. Look for furniture with built-in storage, such as ottomans or beds with drawers. Use clear storage bins to maximize space and keep items visible.
