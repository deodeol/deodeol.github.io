How to Make Philadelphia Cream Cheese Cheesecake Bites

While Philadelphia Cream Cheese Cheesecake Bites are readily available at most grocery stores, you can also make them at home if you prefer. To make your own homemade Philadelphia Cream Cheese Cheesecake Bites, you will need Philadelphia cream cheese, graham cracker crumbs, sugar, melted butter, and your choice of toppings. Simply mix the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter together to create the crust, press it into a baking dish, and then pour the cream cheese filling on top. Bake in the oven until the cheesecake is set, and then let it cool before cutting it into bite-sized pieces. Add your favorite toppings, and you're ready to enjoy your homemade Philadelphia Cream Cheese Cheesecake Bites.
