Question and Answer

Q1: What is the best time to visit Koh Samui for the beach?
A1: The best time to visit Koh Samui for the beach is during the dry season, which runs from December to February. During this time, the weather is sunny and the seas are calm, making it perfect for beach activities.
Q2: Are the beaches in Koh Samui crowded?
A2: The level of crowding on the beaches in Koh Samui can vary depending on the time of year and the specific beach. While some beaches can get crowded, especially during peak tourist season, there are also plenty of quieter and more secluded beaches to choose from.
