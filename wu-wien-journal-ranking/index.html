What is WU Wien Journal Ranking?

WU Wien Journal Ranking is a comprehensive ranking system developed by the Vienna University of Economics and Business (WU Wien). It evaluates and ranks academic journals based on various criteria such as citation impact, publication quality, and international visibility. The ranking covers a wide range of disciplines and provides researchers with valuable information to identify the most reputable journals in their field.

History and Myth of WU Wien Journal Ranking

The WU Wien Journal Ranking was first introduced in 2009 and has since become one of the most influential journal ranking systems in the world. Its methodology is based on a combination of citation data, peer review evaluations, and expert opinions. Over the years, the ranking has gained a reputation for its rigor and objectivity, making it a trusted resource for researchers worldwide.

Understanding the WU Wien Journal Ranking Criteria

The WU Wien Journal Ranking evaluates journals based on several criteria, including citation impact, publication quality, and international visibility. Citation impact measures the influence of a journal's articles by analyzing the number of times they are cited by other researchers. Publication quality assesses the rigor and quality of the journal's peer review process. International visibility considers the journal's readership and reach across different countries and regions.
