What is Independence Day Film Series Movies?

The Independence Day film series movies consist of two science fiction action films directed by Roland Emmerich. The first film, "Independence Day," was released in 1996 and became a blockbuster hit. The second film, "Independence Day: Resurgence," was released in 2016 as a sequel to the original film. Both movies follow the story of humanity's fight against an alien invasion and showcase epic battles, stunning visual effects, and a cast of memorable characters.
