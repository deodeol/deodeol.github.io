The History and Myth of Independence Day Indonesia Pinterest

Independence Day Indonesia, also known as Hari Kemerdekaan, is celebrated on August 17th every year. It commemorates Indonesia's declaration of independence from Dutch colonial rule in 1945. The day is filled with various activities and events, including flag-raising ceremonies, parades, and cultural performances.

In addition to its historical significance, Independence Day Indonesia is also surrounded by myths and legends. One popular myth is the story of the Proclamation Room, where Indonesia's first president, Soekarno, and vice president, Mohammad Hatta, declared independence. According to the myth, the room was chosen for its mystical powers and was believed to bring good fortune to the nation.
