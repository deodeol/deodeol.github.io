What is WU Vienna Campus?

The WU Vienna Campus is the main campus of the Vienna University of Economics and Business, also known as Wirtschaftsuniversität Wien. Established in 1898, the university has a long history of providing excellent education in the fields of business, economics, and law. The WU Vienna Campus is located in the second district of Vienna, known as Leopoldstadt, and covers an area of approximately 90,000 square meters. The campus features modern architecture and state-of-the-art facilities, providing students with an optimal learning environment.
