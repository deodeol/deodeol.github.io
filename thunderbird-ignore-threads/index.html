Question and Answer

Q: Can I un-ignore a thread in Thunderbird?

A: Yes, you can un-ignore a thread in Thunderbird by simply moving the message from the trash folder back to your inbox. This will restore the thread and any future messages in it will once again appear in your inbox.

Q: Will ignoring a thread in Thunderbird delete the messages?

A: No, ignoring a thread in Thunderbird will not delete the messages. They will simply be moved to the trash folder. You can easily retrieve them from the trash folder if needed.

Q: Can I ignore multiple threads in Thunderbird at once?

A: Yes, you can ignore multiple threads in Thunderbird at once. Simply select the threads you want to ignore, right-click, and choose the "Ignore Threads" option from the context menu.

Q: Will ignoring a thread in Thunderbird affect other email clients?

A: No, ignoring a thread in Thunderbird will only affect Thunderbird itself. Other email clients or webmail interfaces will not be affected, and the ignored thread will still appear in those clients.
