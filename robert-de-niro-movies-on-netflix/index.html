2. "Raging Bull" - Directed by Martin Scorsese, this biographical sports drama tells the story of boxer Jake LaMotta, played by De Niro. The film showcases De Niro's dedication to his craft, as he gained weight and trained extensively to portray LaMotta at different stages of his life.

3. "Goodfellas" - Another collaboration between De Niro and Scorsese, this crime drama follows the rise and fall of mobster Henry Hill, played by Ray Liotta. De Niro's performance as Jimmy Conway is a standout, and the film is widely regarded as one of the greatest of all time.
