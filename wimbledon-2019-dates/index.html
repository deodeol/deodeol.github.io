The Wimbledon 2019 dates are from July 1st to July 14th. This two-week-long tournament is held annually in London, England. The tournament attracts the top tennis players from around the world, making it a highly competitive and exciting event to watch.

The history of Wimbledon dates back to 1877 when the first tournament took place. Since then, it has grown in popularity and has become a staple in the tennis calendar.
