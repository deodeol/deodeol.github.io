2. Work on Your Serve and Volley:

Grass courts favor players who have a strong serve and volley game. Practice your serve to ensure accuracy and power. Work on your volley technique to be able to finish points at the net. Mastering these skills will give you an edge on grass courts.

Grass courts reward players who can anticipate shots and position themselves well on the court. Focus on reading your opponent's shots and moving efficiently to cover the court. Being in the right position will allow you to hit offensive shots and put pressure on your opponent.

4. Embrace the Unpredictability:

Grass courts can be unpredictable, with the ball skidding and bouncing differently compared to other surfaces. Embrace the challenge and adapt your game accordingly. Stay light on your feet, be prepared for low and skidding shots, and stay focused throughout the match.
