What does the idiom "Cry Over Spilt Milk" Mean in Bengali?

The idiom "Cry Over Spilt Milk" means to be upset or remorseful about something that has already happened and cannot be changed. It is used to advise someone not to waste time and energy worrying or being upset about past mistakes or misfortunes, as it is futile and unproductive. In Bengali, this idiom can be translated as "বিষণ্ণ হত্যা বা কোন অপ্রয়োজনীয় বিষয় নিয়ে মনে খারাপ হওয়া" (bishanno hattya ba kon apryojoniy bishoy niye mone kharap hoya).
