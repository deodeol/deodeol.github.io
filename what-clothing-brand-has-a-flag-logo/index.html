The Clothing Brand with a Flag Logo

The clothing brand that has a flag logo is none other than Tommy Hilfiger. Tommy Hilfiger is an American fashion brand that is known for its classic and preppy style. Their logo features a flag with red and white stripes, representing the brand's American heritage.

History and Myth of Tommy Hilfiger

Tommy Hilfiger was founded in 1985 by fashion designer Tommy Hilfiger. The brand quickly gained popularity for its unique take on American fashion, blending classic styles with a modern twist. Over the years, Tommy Hilfiger has become synonymous with quality and timeless fashion.

Hidden Secret of Tommy Hilfiger

One interesting fact about Tommy Hilfiger is that the brand's logo was inspired by the American flag. The red and white stripes on the logo represent the flag's stripes, while the blue rectangle in the upper left corner symbolizes the union. This hidden secret adds an extra layer of meaning to the brand's logo.
