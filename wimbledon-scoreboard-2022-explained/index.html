The first player to win four points wins the game, but if both players reach 40 points, it is known as a deuce, and they must win by two clear points.

The Wimbledon Scoreboard 2022 also displays the set score, which represents the number of sets won by each player. A set consists of a series of games, and the player who wins six games with a margin of two or more games wins the set. If the set score reaches 6-6, a tiebreaker is played to determine the winner of the set.
