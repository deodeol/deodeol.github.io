Question and Answer

Q: Can I return my rental car outside of regular business hours at Philadelphia Airport?

Q: Is there a fee for returning a rental car at Philadelphia Airport?

A: Rental car companies may charge a fee for returning a vehicle at Philadelphia Airport. This fee is typically included in the rental agreement or disclosed at the time of booking. Q: Can I return a rental car to a different location than Philadelphia Airport?

A: Some rental car companies allow you to return a vehicle to a different location than the one where you picked it up, known as a one-way rental. However, this may incur additional fees or restrictions. Check with your rental car company for their policies regarding one-way rentals.

Q: What happens if I return my rental car late at Philadelphia Airport?

A: Returning a rental car late at Philadelphia Airport may result in additional charges. Rental car companies typically have a grace period for late returns, but after that, you may be charged for an additional day or a late return fee. It's best to communicate with your rental car company if you anticipate being late to avoid any surprises.
