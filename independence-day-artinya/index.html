What is Independence Day Artinya?

Independence Day Artinya, also known as Artinya Kemerdekaan, is a national holiday in Indonesia that commemorates the country's independence from colonial rule. On August 17th, 1945, Indonesia declared its independence after years of struggle against Dutch colonialism. This day is celebrated with various cultural events, parades, flag hoisting ceremonies, and fireworks displays. It is a time for Indonesians to come together and celebrate their freedom and unity as a nation.
