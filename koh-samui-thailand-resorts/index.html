History and Myth of Koh Samui Thailand Resorts

Over the years, Koh Samui has seen its fair share of changes. Once a sleepy fishing village, the island has transformed into a popular tourist destination, known for its stunning beaches and luxury resorts. Despite the development, Koh Samui has managed to retain its natural beauty and charm.
