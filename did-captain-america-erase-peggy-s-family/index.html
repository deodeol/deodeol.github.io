The Hidden Secret of Captain America's Decision

While Captain America's decision to stay in the past may seem like a happy ending, there is a hidden secret behind his choice. In the Marvel Cinematic Universe, time travel operates on a "closed loop" system, meaning that any changes made in the past have already happened in the future.

This hidden secret adds a layer of complexity to Captain America's decision and further emphasizes the importance of his relationship with Peggy.
