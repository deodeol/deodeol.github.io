History and Myth of Wimbledon Weather Forecast Tennis

The importance of the weather in Wimbledon dates back to its early years. The tournament, which started in 1877, was originally played on an outdoor grass court. This has led to the development of retractable roofs on the Centre Court and No. 1 Court, which allow matches to continue even in adverse weather conditions.
