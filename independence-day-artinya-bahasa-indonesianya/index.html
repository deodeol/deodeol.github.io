Independence Day in Indonesia is celebrated on August 17th and commemorates the country's proclamation of independence from Dutch colonial rule in 1945. The proclamation of independence marked the beginning of a new era for Indonesia as a sovereign nation.

There are various myths and stories surrounding the struggle for independence in Indonesia. One of the most well-known is the story of the national hero, Bung Tomo.
