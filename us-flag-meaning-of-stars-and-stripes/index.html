Understanding the Meaning of Stars and Stripes on the US Flag

The stars and stripes on the US flag hold great significance and represent various aspects of our nation. The stars symbolize the 50 states of the United States, while the stripes represent the original 13 colonies that declared independence from Great Britain.

The US flag has a rich history dating back to the American Revolution. The flag has evolved over the years, with the stars and stripes becoming the official design in 1818. It is said that Betsy Ross, a seamstress from Philadelphia, was the one who sewed the first American flag with 13 stars and stripes.
