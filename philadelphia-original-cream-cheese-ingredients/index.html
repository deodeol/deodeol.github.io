The History and Myth of Philadelphia Original Cream Cheese

Philadelphia Cream Cheese has a long and storied history. It was first created in 1872 by a dairy farmer named William Lawrence. Lawrence accidentally discovered the method of creating cream cheese while trying to replicate a French cheese called Neufchâtel. He found that by adding cream to the cheese curds, he could create a smoother and creamier texture. The cheese became popular in Philadelphia, hence the name "Philadelphia Cream Cheese". Over the years, Philadelphia Cream Cheese has become synonymous with quality and has remained a favorite among cream cheese lovers.
