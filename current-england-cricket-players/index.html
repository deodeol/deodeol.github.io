One of the most famous myths surrounding England cricket players is the "Ashes" series. The Ashes is a biennial Test cricket series played between England and Australia. The series is named after a satirical obituary published in a British newspaper in 1882, which stated that English cricket had died and "the body will be cremated and the ashes taken to Australia." Since then, the Ashes series has become one of the most fiercely contested rivalries in cricket.
